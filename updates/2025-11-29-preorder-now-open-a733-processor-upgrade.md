## Technical Highlights

The A733 processor brings professional-grade performance to Pocket PC:

- **CPU**: Octa-core ARM (2x Cortex-A76 @ 2.0 GHz + 6x Cortex-A55)
- **GPU**: Imagination BXM-4-64 MC1 with Vulkan 1.3 and OpenGL ES 3.2
- **Video**: 8K@24fps H.265/VP9 decode, 4K@30fps H.264/H.265 encode
- **Display**: HDMI 2.0b and DisplayPort 1.4, both supporting 4K@60fps
- **Connectivity**: USB 3.1 Gen 2, Gigabit Ethernet (via adapter)
